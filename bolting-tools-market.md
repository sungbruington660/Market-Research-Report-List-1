<p><p>The bolting tools market comprises several types, each serving specific application needs. Mechanical bolting tools rely on manual force or torque for fastening, suitable for light-duty tasks. Hydraulic tools use pressurized fluid to generate higher torque, ideal for heavy industrial applications. Pneumatic tools operate with compressed air, providing power and efficiency for medium to high torque requirements. Electric bolting tools combine portability with consistent torque output, making them versatile for various tasks in construction and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
