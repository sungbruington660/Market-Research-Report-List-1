<p><p>Digital magnifiers are assistive devices designed to enhance the readability of text and other visual information for individuals with low vision. They can range from handheld devices to desktop models, often featuring adjustable magnification levels, contrast settings, and the ability to capture and store images. </p><p>The Digital Magnifiers Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by an increasing aging population and a rising prevalence of vision impairments. Trends such as the integration of connectivity features, compatibility with smartphones, and the exploration of artificial intelligence for enhanced image processing are gaining traction.
