<p><ul><li>Non-Immersive Type</li><li>Semi-Physical Type</li><li>Totally Immersive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Virtual Reality Devices market is categorized into three main types: Non-Immersive, Semi-Physical, and Totally Immersive. Non-Immersive types offer basic interactions through screens without full engagement, often seen in gaming on PCs or consoles. Semi-Physical types combine real-world elements with VR, allowing limited interaction through motion control or handheld devices. Totally Immersive devices use specialized headsets and motion tracking to create a fully engaging environment, enabling users to experience virtual worlds as if they were physically present.</p></p>
