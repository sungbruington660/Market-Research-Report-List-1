<p><p>An air hose is a flexible tube designed to transport compressed air from a source, such as an air compressor, to various tools and equipment. Air hoses are typically made from materials like rubber, PVC, or polyurethane, offering durability, flexibility, and resistance to abrasion and extreme temperatures.</p><p>The Air Hose Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing industrialization, the rise in demand for pneumatic tools, and advancements in hose materials and technology.
