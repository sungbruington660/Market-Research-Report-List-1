<p><p>The advanced utility boiler market comprises several types: Subcritical boilers operate below critical pressure, offering cost-effectiveness but lower efficiency. Supercritical boilers exceed critical pressure, improving efficiency and reducing emissions, making them a preferred choice in modern power plants. Ultra-supercritical boilers take efficiency further by operating at even higher temperatures and pressures, minimizing fuel consumption and emissions. Other types may include integrated designs or specialized boilers for specific fuels and applications, catering to diverse energy needs and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
