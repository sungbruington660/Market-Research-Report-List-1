<p><ul><li>Clipped Head Framing Nailer</li><li>Round Head Framing Nailer</li><li>Coil Framing Nailer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The framing nailer market includes three main types: Clipped Head, Round Head, and Coil framing nailers. Clipped Head nailers are designed for increased speed and efficiency, with a compact profile ideal for tight spaces, though they may not be compatible with all building codes. Round Head nailers are more traditional, providing stronger holding power, making them suitable for structural applications. Coil framing nailers utilize a continuous strip of nails, enabling high-volume work with less frequent reloading, perfect for large projects.</p></p>
