Hydrophilic solvents, which have a strong attraction to water molecules, are utilized in applications requiring moisture retention and compatibility with water-based systems. In contrast, hydrophobic solvents repel water and are preferred in conditions demanding water resistance, such as coatings and sealants. Each type plays a critical role in various industries, including coatings, adhesives, and electronics, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
