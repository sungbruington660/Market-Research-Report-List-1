LSR is ideal for high-volume production and intricate designs, offering excellent biocompatibility and flexibility. HCR, known for its robust mechanical properties, is suitable for applications requiring durability and strength. LCR typically refers to a lower viscosity silicone used in medical devices needing precise detailing. Each type serves distinct applications within the biomedical sector, driven by specific performance requirements.</p></p>
