<p><p>The few-mode fibers market is categorized primarily into 2-mode, 4-mode, and other modes. 2-mode fibers support two distinct light paths, enhancing bandwidth and reducing signal loss, making them ideal for short-distance communication. 4-mode fibers allow four modes, enabling greater data transmission capabilities and efficiency over longer distances, suitable for advanced networking applications. Other mode configurations include specialized fibers designed for unique applications, catering to specific industry needs, such as sensing and customized telecommunications solutions, driving innovation in optical networks.</p></p>
