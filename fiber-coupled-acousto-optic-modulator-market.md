<p><p>The fiber-coupled acousto-optic modulator (AOM) market is segmented primarily into two types: SM fiber and SMPM fiber. SM fiber, or single-mode fiber, provides high precision and is ideal for applications requiring low optical loss and minimal modal dispersion. In contrast, SMPM fiber, or single-mode polarization-maintaining fiber, preserves the polarization of light, making it suitable for applications where maintaining the state of polarization is critical. Both types cater to diverse applications in telecommunications, sensors, and laser systems.</p></p>
