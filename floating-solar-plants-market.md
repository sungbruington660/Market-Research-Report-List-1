<p><p>Floating solar plants comprise several market types essential for their functioning. PV modules form the core, converting sunlight into electricity. The floating body provides the platform on water bodies, typically made of durable materials to support the solar panels. The anchoring system secures the setup to prevent movement due to waves or winds. Inverters play a crucial role in converting direct current (DC) from the PV modules into alternating current (AC) for grid compatibility. Other components may include cabling and monitoring systems.</p></p>
