<p><ul><li>Electric Spreaders</li><li>Electro-hydraulic Spreaders</li><li>Pneumatic Spreaders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spreader markets are categorized into three main types: electric spreaders, electro-hydraulic spreaders, and pneumatic spreaders. Electric spreaders operate through electric motors, offering precise control and efficiency, ideal for smaller applications. Electro-hydraulic spreaders utilize hydraulic systems powered by electricity, providing powerful performance for heavier loads and larger areas. Pneumatic spreaders rely on air pressure to distribute materials, ensuring quick and even application for various materials like seeds and fertilizers. Each type caters to specific needs in agriculture and construction sectors.</p></p>
