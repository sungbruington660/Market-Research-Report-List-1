<p><p>The paint and urethane stripper market is primarily segmented into alkaline and acidic types. Alkaline strippers utilize caustic ingredients to break down paint and coatings, making them effective on a variety of surfaces, especially wood and metal. Conversely, acidic strippers contain strong acids that dissolve paint by reacting chemically with it, often used for tougher coatings. Each type serves specific applications, with alkaline strippers generally being more environmentally friendly, while acidic options are preferred for heavy-duty removal tasks.</p></p>
