<p><p>The 3D mammography machines market comprises three main types: Computed Radiography (CR) machines, Flat Panel machines, and CMOS Flat machines. CR machines utilize photostimulable phosphor plates to capture images, offering enhanced image quality and reduced radiation exposure. Flat Panel machines employ digital detectors for superior image resolution and faster processing times, improving workflow efficiency. CMOS Flat machines leverage advanced complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, providing high-resolution images with lower power consumption and cost-effective production, making them increasingly popular in breast cancer screening.</p></p>
