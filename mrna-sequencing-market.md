<p><p>mRNA sequencing involves various market types, primarily focusing on PCR, microarray, and sequencing technologies. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific RNA segments, enhancing detection sensitivity. Microarrays enable simultaneous analysis of thousands of RNA transcripts, providing a comprehensive expression profile. In contrast, sequencing technologies, such as RNA-Seq, offer detailed insights into the transcriptome, revealing both quantitative and qualitative information about gene expression. Together, these methods facilitate advancements in genomics, personalized medicine, and biomarker discovery, catering to diverse research and clinical applications.</p></p>
