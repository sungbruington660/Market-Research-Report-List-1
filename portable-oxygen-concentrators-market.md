Macromolecule oxygen permeable membrane concentrators utilize specialized membranes to selectively allow oxygen to pass through. Electrolysis oxygen concentrators generate oxygen via water electrolysis, while chemical oxygen concentrators produce oxygen through chemical reactions. Other market types may include advanced technologies or hybrid systems, catering to varying needs in oxygen therapy and mobility.</p></p>
