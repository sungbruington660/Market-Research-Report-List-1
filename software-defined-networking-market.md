<p><p>Software-Defined Networking (SDN) is an approach to network management that allows for the dynamic adjustment of network resources through centralized control software. By decoupling the network control plane from the data plane, SDN enhances network flexibility, scalability, and automation. This technology enables administrators to manage traffic and network configurations efficiently, facilitating quicker responses to evolving business needs.</p><p>The Software-Defined Networking Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period. Factors contributing to this growth include the rising demand for efficient network management, increased adoption of cloud services, and the necessity for enhanced data center management. Organizations are increasingly recognizing the benefits of SDN in optimizing bandwidth usage and minimizing capital expenditure, leading to wider adoption across various industries.</p><p>Latest trends in the SDN market include the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) for predictive analytics and enhanced decision-making processes, as well as advancements in security protocols to combat growing cyber threats.
