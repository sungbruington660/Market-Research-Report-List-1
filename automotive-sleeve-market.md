<p><strong>The Automotive Sleeve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Type Sleeve</li><li>Wet-Type Sleeve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive sleeve market comprises two main types: dry-type and wet-type sleeves. Dry-type sleeves are installed directly into the engine block without coolant contact, offering durability and reduced friction. They are commonly used in high-performance engines. Wet-type sleeves, on the other hand, are in direct contact with engine coolant, providing better heat dissipation and easier replacement. They are often used in heavy-duty or commercial vehicles. Both types enhance engine performance and longevity, catering to different automotive needs and applications.</p></p>
