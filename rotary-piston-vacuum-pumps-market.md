<p><p>The rotary piston vacuum pumps market is segmented into single-stage and two-stage designs. Single-stage rotary piston vacuum pumps operate with one impeller, achieving moderate vacuum levels, making them suitable for applications like packaging and food industries. In contrast, two-stage rotary piston vacuum pumps feature two impellers, allowing for deeper vacuum levels and higher efficiency, ideal for demanding applications such as chemical processing and laboratory use. Both types are valued for reliability, durability, and versatility in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
