<p><strong>The Native Salt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sea Salt</li><li>Lake Salt</li><li>Well and Rock Salt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Native Salt market encompasses various types of salt sourced from natural environments. Sea salt is harvested through evaporation of seawater, offering trace minerals and distinct flavors. Lake salt is derived from saline lakes, often with unique compositions. Well salt is extracted from underground salt deposits via drilling, providing a pure form ideal for various uses. Rock salt, or halite, is mined directly from salt deposits and is commonly used for de-icing, food processing, and industrial applications. Each type has unique properties and applications.</p></p>
