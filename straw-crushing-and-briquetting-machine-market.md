<p><p>The straw crushing and briquetting machine market is categorized into three main types: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. Mechanical machines utilize gears and levers to compress straw into briquettes, offering a straightforward and cost-effective option. Hydraulic machines leverage pressure applied by hydraulic systems for efficient and dense briquette production. Screw extrusion machines use a rotating screw to compress and mold straw into briquettes, providing a continuous feed and uniform size, making them suitable for high-volume operations. Each type serves specific needs within the biomass industry.</p></p>
