<p><p>Emission Monitoring Systems (EMS) are critical for environmental compliance and air quality management. Predictive Emission Monitoring Systems (PEMS) utilize advanced algorithms and modeling to estimate emissions based on process parameters and historical data, offering real-time insights without continuous sampling. In contrast, Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems (CEMS) provide real-time, direct measurements of pollutants emitted from industrial sources through continuous sampling and analysis. Both systems play vital roles in regulatory compliance and help industries reduce their environmental impact.</p></p>
