Guard locking switches enhance safety by ensuring that access doors to hazardous areas remain locked when machinery is operating. Hinge switches, installed on access doors or panels, detect position and enable or disable machinery operation based on door status. Other types may include safety switches, non-contact switches, and emergency stop switches, all designed to enhance operational safety and prevent unauthorized access in industrial settings.</p></p>
