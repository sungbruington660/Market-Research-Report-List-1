<p><p>Fixed abrasive refers to abrasive materials that are permanently bonded to a substrate, such as grinding wheels, polishing pads, or diamond cutting tools. Unlike loose abrasives, fixed abrasives offer higher efficiency, consistency, and durability, making them ideal for high-precision applications.</p><p>The Fixed Abrasive Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
