<p><strong>The Maritime Thermal Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The maritime thermal cameras market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal cameras. Uncooled type cameras operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing microbolometer technology, making them compact and cost-effective. They offer adequate performance for general maritime surveillance. In contrast, cooled type cameras require a cooling mechanism to enhance sensitivity, delivering superior image quality and detection range. These are ideal for high-demand scenarios like naval operations, search and rescue, and critical threat detection in challenging environments.</p></p>
