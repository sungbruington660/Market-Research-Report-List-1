<p><strong>The Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The copper foil market comprises two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through a mechanical process, offering superior thickness precision and surface quality, making it ideal for applications in electronics and flexible printed circuits. In contrast, electrolytic copper foil is manufactured via an electrochemical process, resulting in thinner materials with excellent conductivity, often used in batteries and high-performance electronic devices. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, driving market demand.</p></p>
