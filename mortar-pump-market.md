<p><ul><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Pnuematic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mortar pump market consists of two primary types: hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic mortar pumps utilize liquid pressure to transport materials, offering high power and efficiency, making them ideal for large-scale construction projects. Conversely, pneumatic mortar pumps use compressed air to convey materials, providing flexibility and ease of use in smaller or confined spaces. Both types cater to different application needs, with hydraulic pumps favoring heavy-duty tasks, while pneumatic pumps excel in portability and simplicity for lighter jobs.</p></p>
