<p><p>The PVDC dispersion market is categorized into three viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity PVDC dispersions are typically used for applications requiring easy processing and quick drying, often in coatings and adhesives. Medium viscosity blends offer a balance between ease of application and film strength, making them suitable for packaging films. High viscosity PVDC dispersions provide enhanced thickness and barrier properties, ideal for demanding applications like food packaging and specialty coatings, ensuring superior performance and durability.</p></p>
