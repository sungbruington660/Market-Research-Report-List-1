<p><ul><li>Slewing Ring Bearings</li><li>Spherical Roller Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power bearing market includes key types like slewing ring bearings and spherical roller bearings, essential for wind turbine functionality. Slewing ring bearings support rotational movement in yaw and pitch systems, enabling turbines to align with wind direction and optimize blade angles. Spherical roller bearings handle high radial and axial loads in gearboxes and generators, ensuring smooth operation under dynamic conditions. Both types are critical for durability, efficiency, and reliability in wind energy systems, driving demand in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
