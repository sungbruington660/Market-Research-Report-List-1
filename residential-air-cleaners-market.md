<p><p>The residential air cleaners market encompasses various types designed to improve indoor air quality. HEPA filters trap small particles, offering high efficiency in capturing dust and allergens. Activated carbon filters absorb odors and volatile organic compounds. Electrostatic precipitators use charged plates to collect particles, while ionizers release negative ions to neutralize airborne pollutants. Ozone generators produce ozone to purify air, although they can produce harmful byproducts. Other types may include UV light purifiers and photocatalytic devices, each catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
