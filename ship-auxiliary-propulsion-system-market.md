<p><p>The Ship Auxiliary Propulsion System market includes two main types: Controllable Pitch Propeller (CPP) and Fixed Pitch Propeller (FPP). CPP systems allow for adjustable blade angles, optimizing thrust and efficiency in varying conditions, which enhances maneuverability and fuel efficiency. In contrast, FPP systems have a constant blade angle, providing simplicity and reliability but less operational flexibility. Both types serve to enhance ship performance, reduce fuel consumption, and improve overall operational efficiency in maritime applications.</p></p>
