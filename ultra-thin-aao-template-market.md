<p><p>The ultra-thin anodic aluminum oxide (AAO) template market can be categorized into single-pass and double-pass types. In contrast, double-pass templates utilize two anodization stages, enhancing the pore density and uniformity, ideal for advanced research purposes and applications requiring higher precision. Both types cater to distinct needs within industries like electronics and biotechnology.</p></p>
