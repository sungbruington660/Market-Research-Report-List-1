<p><p>Single-head stethoscopes are medical instruments used for auscultation, primarily designed to listen to internal body sounds such as heartbeats, lung sounds, and bowel noises. Unlike their dual-head counterparts, which feature both a diaphragm and a bell, single-head stethoscopes typically have only a diaphragm, providing a more focused acoustic experience, making them a preferred choice for certain clinical situations.</p><p>The Single-Head Stethoscopes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in clinical settings and a growing emphasis on diagnostic accuracy.
