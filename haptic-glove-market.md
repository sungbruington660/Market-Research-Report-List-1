<p><p>The haptic glove market can be categorized into two main types: Force Feedback Haptic Gloves and Precision Hand Tracking Haptic Gloves. In contrast, Precision Hand Tracking Haptic Gloves focus on accurately capturing hand movements and gestures, providing real-time feedback for applications like gaming and VR training. Both types aim to enhance user experience through realistic interaction with digital elements.</p></p>
