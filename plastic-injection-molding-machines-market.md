<p><p>Plastic injection molding machines are categorized into three main types: electric, hydraulic, and hybrid. Electric machines offer precise control, energy efficiency, and lower operating costs, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Hydraulic machines rely on fluid power for clamping and injection, providing strong force and larger shot sizes, suitable for heavy-duty production. Hybrid machines combine both technologies, leveraging the efficiency of electric systems with the robust power of hydraulic systems, catering to various manufacturing needs and enhancing productivity.</p></p>
