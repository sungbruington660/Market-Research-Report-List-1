<p><ul><li>Traditional PET-MRI Systems</li><li>Helium-free PET-MRI Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Brain PET MRI Imaging Systems market includes two primary types: traditional PET-MRI systems and helium-free PET-MRI systems. Traditional systems utilize liquid helium to cool the MRI magnets, ensuring high-quality imaging but raising concerns about sustainability and resource costs. In contrast, helium-free systems employ alternative cooling methods, which reduce environmental impact and operational costs while maintaining diagnostic performance. Both types are vital for advanced neuroimaging, enabling comprehensive insights into brain function and pathology for clinical and research applications.</p></p>
