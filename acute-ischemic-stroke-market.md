<p><p>The Acute Ischemic Stroke market is categorized into three main therapeutic types. Antihypertensive therapeutics focus on managing high blood pressure to reduce stroke risk and improve outcomes. Antiplatelet therapeutics help prevent blood clots by inhibiting platelet aggregation, crucial for patients post-stroke. Anticoagulants are aimed at preventing thrombus formation in high-risk patients, enhancing recovery and preventing further strokes. Each of these therapeutic categories plays a vital role in the overall management and treatment of Acute Ischemic Stroke.</p></p>
