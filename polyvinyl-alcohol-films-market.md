<p><ul><li>Quick Dissolving Film</li><li>Dissolved Film</li><li>Undissolved Film</li><li>Special Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyvinyl Alcohol (PVA) films are categorized into Quick Dissolving Films, Dissolved Films, Undissolved Films, and Special Films. Quick Dissolving Films dissolve rapidly in water, ideal for packaging detergents or pharmaceuticals. Dissolved Films break down completely in water, used in applications like water-soluble laundry bags. Undissolved Films remain intact in water, suitable for packaging or industrial uses. Special Films are engineered for unique properties like enhanced strength, barrier resistance, or biodegradability, catering to niche applications in industries such as agriculture or medical packaging.</p></p>
