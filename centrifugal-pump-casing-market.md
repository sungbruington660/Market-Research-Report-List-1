<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Centrifugal Pump Casing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Volute Casing or Spiral Casing</li><li>Vortex Casing</li><li>Casing With Guide Blades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal pump casing types include volute or spiral casing, vortex casing, and casing with guide blades. Volute casings feature a curved channel that collects and directs fluid flow, enhancing efficiency. Vortex casings create a low-pressure zone to minimize turbulence, suitable for handling solids or slurries. Casing with guide blades incorporates strategically placed blades to streamline fluid flow, improving performance and stability. Each type is designed to optimize the pump's operation based on specific application and fluid characteristics.</p></p>
