<p><ul><li>Homogeneity Rubber Floor Covering</li><li>Heterogeneous Rubber Floor Covering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber floor covering market is categorized into two main types: homogeneous and heterogeneous. Homogeneous rubber flooring is made from a single material composition, ensuring consistent color and texture throughout, which enhances durability and simplifies maintenance. In contrast, heterogeneous rubber flooring consists of multiple layers, combining different materials to achieve varied aesthetics, cushioning, and performance characteristics. This type often features decorative patterns and improved shock absorption, making it suitable for diverse applications in commercial and residential spaces.</p></p>
