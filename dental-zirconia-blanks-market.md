<p><p>The dental zirconia blanks market comprises two main types: monolayer and multilayer zirconia blanks. Monolayer zirconia blanks consist of a single, uniform layer, providing consistent strength and aesthetics, ideal for simple dental restorations. In contrast, multilayer zirconia blanks feature multiple layers, each with varying translucency and color gradients, resembling natural teeth more closely. Multilayer options are increasingly preferred for complex restorations, as they offer enhanced visual appeal and customized solutions for dental professionals, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
