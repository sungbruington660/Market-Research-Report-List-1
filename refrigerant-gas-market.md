<p><p>The refrigerant gas market includes types like HCFC-22, R134a, and R404a, each serving distinct applications. R134a, a hydrofluorocarbon, is common in automotive air conditioning and domestic refrigeration, valued for its lower environmental impact. R404a, a blend of HFCs, is used in commercial refrigeration for its efficiency but faces restrictions due to high global warming potential. All are transitioning to eco-friendly alternatives.</p></p>
