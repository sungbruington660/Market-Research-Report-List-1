<p><p>The disintegrants market is segmented into natural and synthetic types, each serving a critical role in pharmaceutical formulations. Both types are essential for optimizing drug release profiles, improving bioavailability, and ensuring effective delivery of active pharmaceutical ingredients.</p></p>
