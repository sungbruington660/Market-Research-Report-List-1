<p><p>The ice cream filling machine market primarily consists of two types: rotary and linear machines. Rotary ice cream filling machines are designed for high-speed production, featuring multiple filling heads that operate in a circular motion, allowing for efficient handling of large volumes. In contrast, linear ice cream filling machines operate in a straight line, offering flexibility and ease of use for smaller batches or varying product sizes. Both types are essential for streamlining the filling process in the ice cream manufacturing industry.</p></p>
