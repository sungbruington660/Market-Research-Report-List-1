<p><p>The Flame Resistant and Retardant Fabric market comprises two main types: **Inherent Flame Resistant and Retardant Fabric** and **Treated Flame Resistant and Retardant Fabric**. Inherent fabrics are made from fibers that are naturally resistant to flames, offering long-lasting protection without chemical treatments. Treated fabrics, on the other hand, are chemically coated or finished to achieve flame resistance, which may diminish over time with washing or wear. Both types are used in industries requiring fire safety, such as oil, gas, and construction.</p></p>
