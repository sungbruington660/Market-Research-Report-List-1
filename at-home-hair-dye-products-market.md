<p><p>The at-home hair dye products market is categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Temporary hair dyes provide short-lived color that typically lasts until the next wash, offering a fun and versatile option. Semi-permanent dyes penetrate the hair shaft slightly, lasting several weeks before fading. Permanent hair dyes create long-lasting color by chemically altering the hair structure, providing a more durable solution for those seeking a lasting change. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
