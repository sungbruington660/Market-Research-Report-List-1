<p><p>The marine thrusters market comprises various types, primarily Controllable Pitch Propellers (CPP) and Fixed Pitch Propellers (FPP). CPPs allow for adjustable blade angles, enhancing maneuverability and efficiency across different speeds, making them ideal for dynamic marine operations. In contrast, FPPs have fixed blades optimized for specific conditions, providing simplicity and reliability, often used in vessels operating at constant speeds. Both types cater to diverse maritime applications, including commercial shipping, offshore support, and recreational boating.</p></p>
