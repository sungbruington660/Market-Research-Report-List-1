<p><p>A steam ejector is a mechanical device that utilizes steam to create a vacuum or move gases and liquids. It operates based on the principle of momentum transfer, where high-pressure steam expands and accelerates through a nozzle, creating a low-pressure zone that draws in the surrounding fluid.
