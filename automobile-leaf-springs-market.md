<p><p>The automobile leaf springs market is primarily categorized into two types: monolithic and multiple slices. Monolithic leaf springs are single-piece structures that provide durability and strength, commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles. Multiple slice leaf springs consist of several layers or slices, allowing for enhanced flexibility and better weight distribution, making them suitable for a wider range of vehicles. Both types serve crucial roles in vehicle suspension systems, improving ride comfort and stability under varying loads and road conditions.</p></p>
