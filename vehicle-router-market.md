<p><p>The Vehicle Router market can be categorized into two primary types: Cellular Connection and Wireless Connection. Cellular Connection routers utilize mobile networks to ensure constant internet access for vehicles, facilitating real-time data transfer and communication. In contrast, Wireless Connection routers establish local networks, using Wi-Fi or similar technologies to connect multiple devices within the vehicle. Both types enhance connectivity, improve fleet management, and support various applications such as navigation, vehicle tracking, and infotainment systems tailored for modern transportation needs.</p></p>
