<p><p>The polyolefin resins market is primarily categorized into three types: polyethylene (PE), polypropylene (PP), and others. PE is widely used in packaging, containers, and films due to its excellent chemical resistance and flexibility. PP offers higher thermal stability and is favored in automotive parts, textiles, and consumer goods. The "others" category includes specialty polyolefins like ethylene-vinyl acetate (EVA) and cyclic olefin copolymers (COC), which are utilized in applications ranging from adhesives to advanced medical devices, reflecting versatility and innovation in the market.</p></p>
