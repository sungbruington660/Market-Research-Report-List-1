<p><p>Gaming motherboards are categorized mainly into two types: BTX and ATX. ATX (Advanced Technology eXtended) is the most common form factor, offering extensive expandability, efficient airflow, and compatibility with various components, making it ideal for high-performance gaming rigs. In contrast, BTX (Balanced Technology eXtended) was developed to enhance thermal management and improve airflow but has become less popular in the gaming community. While ATX remains the dominant choice for gamers, BTX offers unique cooling advantages for specific builds.</p></p>
