<p><ul><li>Roller Hearth Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace</li><li>Push Plate Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas carburizing furnace market is primarily categorized into Roller Hearth Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnaces and Push Plate Continuous Gas Carburizing Furnaces. Roller Hearth furnaces utilize a rotating conveyor system, allowing for continuous operation and efficient processing of components, ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, Push Plate furnaces employ a fixed plate mechanism to push components through the carburizing zone, providing precise control over processing times and temperatures, suitable for varied production requirements and small to medium batch sizes.</p></p>
