Crystal type duplexers utilize piezoelectric crystals for frequency selection, offering high stability and low loss, ideal for communication systems. Ceramic type duplexers leverage the properties of ceramic materials, providing compact size and superior performance in harsh environments. Other types include various hybrid configurations and technologies designed to meet specific application requirements, ensuring versatility in telecommunications, radar systems, and satellite communication.</p></p>
