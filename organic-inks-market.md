Water-based inks are eco-friendly, using water as a solvent, suitable for food packaging and textiles. Oil-based inks, derived from vegetable oils, offer vibrant colors and are used in printing applications like newspapers. Other types include UV-curable and bio-based inks, catering to niche and sustainable demands.</p></p>
