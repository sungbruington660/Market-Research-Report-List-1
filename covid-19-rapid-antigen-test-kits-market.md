<p><p>The COVID-19 Rapid Antigen Test Kits market comprises various sample collection types, each suited for detecting the virus. Nasopharyngeal swabs collect samples from deep within the nasal cavity, offering high sensitivity. Nasal swabs, easier to administer, collect samples from the front of the nose. Oropharyngeal swabs sample the back of the throat and are effective but less common. Saliva swabs provide a non-invasive alternative, allowing for easier self-collection while maintaining reasonable accuracy. Each type serves specific needs in testing convenience and efficiency.</p></p>
