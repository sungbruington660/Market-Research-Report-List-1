<p><p>The mobile ventilator market comprises three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators utilize advanced microprocessors for precise control and monitoring, making them suitable for complex patient needs. Pneumatic ventilators operate using compressed air, offering simplicity and reliability, often preferred in emergency settings. Electro-pneumatic systems combine both technologies, leveraging electronic controls with pneumatic efficiency, providing versatility and adaptability for various clinical environments. Each type caters to different patient requirements and operational contexts in respiratory care.</p></p>
