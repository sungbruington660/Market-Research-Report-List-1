<p><p>The Nano Electromechanical System (NEMS) market encompasses various types, including nanotubes, nanowires, nanofilms, and nanobelts, each serving unique applications. Nanotubes are cylindrical structures with excellent electrical and mechanical properties, suitable for sensors and transistors. Nanowires, with their high surface area, are ideal for sensors and energy storage devices. Nanofilms offer thin layers for coatings or barriers, while nanobelts are flexible and used in electronics and photonics. Other materials include nanosheets and nanoparticles, expanding NEMS applications further.</p></p>
